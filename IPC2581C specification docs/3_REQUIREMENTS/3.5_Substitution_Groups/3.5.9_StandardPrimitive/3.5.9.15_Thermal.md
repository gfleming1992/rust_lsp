# 3.5.9.15 Thermal

A Thermal is a StandardPrimitive shape used to remove material from a plane, conductive fill area, or around a plated through hole. Shapes include square, round, hexagonal, or octagonal, with varying numbers of spokes. The center is the point of origin.

| Attribute / Element Name | Attribute / Element Type | Description | Occurrence |
|-------------------------|-------------------------|-------------|------------|
| Thermal                 | ThermalType             | ROUND, SQUARE, HEXAGON, or OCTAGON shape for material removal. | 1-1 |
| shape                   | thermalShapeType        | Shape of the thermal. One of ROUND, SQUARE, HEXAGON, OCTAGON. | 1-1 |
| outerDiameter           | nonNegativeDoubleType   | Outer boundary of the filled region. | 1-1 |
| innerDiameter           | nonNegativeDoubleType   | Inner boundary of the filled region. | 1-1 |
| spokeCount              | spokeCountType          | Number of cutouts allowed in the shapes. | 0-1 |
| spokeWidth              | nonNegativeDoubleType   | Minimum distance between sides of a spoke cut. | 0-1 |
| spokeStartAngle         | angleType               | Angle for first spoke cut (counterclockwise from x-axis). | 1-1 |
| LineDescGroup           | LineDescGroupType       | Substitution group for LineDesc or LineDescRef. | 0-1 |
| FillDescGroup           | FillDescGroupType       | Substitution group for FillDesc or FillDescRef. | 0-1 |

**Example:**
```xml
<Thermal shape="ROUND" outerDiameter="10.6" innerDiameter="6.0" spokeCount="4" spokeWidth="2.0" spokeStartAngle="45.00">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Thermal>
<Thermal shape="SQUARE" outerDiameter="10.8" innerDiameter="6.0" spokeCount="4" spokeWidth="2.4" spokeStartAngle="0.00">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Thermal>
<Thermal shape="HEXAGON" outerDiameter="10.8" innerDiameter="6.4" spokeCount="4" spokeWidth="2.0" spokeStartAngle="45.00">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Thermal>
<Thermal shape="OCTAGON" outerDiameter="10.6" innerDiameter="6.6" spokeCount="4" spokeWidth="2.0" spokeStartAngle="60.00">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Thermal>
```
