# 3.5.9.5 Donut

A Donut is a StandardPrimitive shape composed of two concentric identical shapes (round, square, hexagon, or octagon) with different sizes. The center is the point of origin.

| Attribute / Element Name | Attribute / Element Type | Description | Occurrence |
|-------------------------|-------------------------|-------------|------------|
| Donut                   | DonutType               | Outer and inner diameter define the shape. | 1-1 |
| shape                   | donutShapeType          | ROUND, SQUARE, HEXAGON, or OCTAGON. | 1-1 |
| outerDiameter           | nonNegativeDoubleType   | Outer boundary of the filled region. | 1-1 |
| innerDiameter           | nonNegativeDoubleType   | Inner boundary of the filled region. | 1-1 |
| LineDescGroup           | LineDescGroupType       | Substitution group for LineDesc or LineDescRef. | 0-1 |
| FillDescGroup           | FillDescGroupType       | Substitution group for FillDesc or FillDescRef. | 0-1 |

**Example:**
```xml
<Donut shape="ROUND" outerDiameter="6.8" innerDiameter="4.8">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Donut>
<Donut shape="SQUARE" outerDiameter="6.8" innerDiameter="5.0">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Donut>
<Donut shape="HEXAGON" outerDiameter="12.4" innerDiameter="10.2">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Donut>
<Donut shape="OCTAGON" outerDiameter="11.60" innerDiameter="10.00">
  <FillDesc fillProperty="FILL">
    <Color r="150" g="0" b="0"/>
  </FillDesc>
</Donut>
```
